The Red Path

As you enter the house, an eerie presence chills you.

Grandma sits up in bed, staring at you with a strange, unnatural grin.

 

"Hello dear, why not come a little closer and let me see what you've brought?"

 

Her voice sounds healthy and clear.

 

Her voice should not sound healthy and clear.

Run home as fast as you can
Say hello to "grandma"
